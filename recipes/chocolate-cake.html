Chocolate Tres Leches Cake

Description

This chocolate tres leches cake is a rich and delicious twist on the classic Mexican dessert. It tastes like you spent all day in the kitchen but a boxed mix makes it so easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package Swiss chocolate cake mix (such as Duncan Hines®)
  • 1 ¼ cups chocolate milk
  • ½ cup canola oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon instant espresso powder
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • ¾ cup half-and-half
  • ¾ cup evaporated milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups heavy whipping cream
